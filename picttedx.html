TEDx PICT

TED is a non-profit organization promoting the spread of innovative ideas, best practices,since its inception in 1984. TED stands for Technology, Entertainment and Design but encompasses everything from science, business, arts to global issues.

TEDxPICT is a group of proactive individuals focussed at providing a platform to bring in various stakeholders together to share the ideas, stories, and innovation. In the context of current fast developing world, sharing of ideas , and experiences is very much necessary for the overall society progress.
