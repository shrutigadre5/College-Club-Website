PICT ART CIRCLE

PICT ART CIRCLE is the Cultural group of PICT college that officially represents the college on various levels of Drama and other Art Form Competition.

PICT Art Cicle also participates in a number of cultural events and competitions like Firodiya Karandak, Purushottam Karandak and has been a winner for a number of times.
