PICT ROBOTICS

Students from all classes and departments are welcome here. PICT Robotics is a place where students can indulge into their curiosity and the passion for robotics and dare to go beyond the boundaries of their academics to explore real-life applications of their knowledge, learn new technologies and be exposed to national level competitions. The students also get to interact with over 90 colleges from all over the country, learn about designs, ideas and methods. Technologies that we use: Pneumatic systems for actuation. Automated systems for robots. Brushless DC motors. High power motor drivers and reliable sensors for autonomous operations. Servo motors for precise movements.
