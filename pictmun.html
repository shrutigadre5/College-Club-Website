PICT MUN

The PICT MUN Club, established in 2016, consists of a group of inquisitive students who are passionate about social, political and economic issues that elude the future of our world. PICT MUN strongly believes in the important role of dialogue and discussion in solving any problem and hence always abides by its motto - 'Think.Discuss.Prosper.' PICT MUN organizes an annual MUN conference and various public speaking events to engage the youth, who are the torchbearers of the future, in stimulating conversations.
